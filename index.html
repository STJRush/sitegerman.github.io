The Course

The book used in the first year of the German course is called Viel Spaß and the book used in the second year and third year is called Viel Spaß 2 The book used for leaving cert is called Leaving Cert Oral German. You will also need 2 small copies and a A4 hardback.

Exams

There are two parts to the German exam for both Junior and Leaving cert. The first part is a Aural exam which is 30 minutes long and a Written exam which is 1 hour and 30 minutes. The full exam is 2 hours long The exams arent very hard if studied hard for. Within the written exam,students must write a letter and a short note.
