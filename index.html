The Course

General Aims:
General educational aims: The general educational aims of the Intermediate Certificate programme in the target language are:-

  1. To contribute to pupils’ awareness of language as a system of communication;

  2. To give pupils an awareness of another culture, and thus a more objective perspective of their own culture;

  3. To contribute to the development in pupils of the capacity to engage in fruitful transactions and interactions with others;

    4. (d) Through (a), (b) and (c) to contribute to pupil’s overall personal and social development;
  4. To give pupils the possibility of access to sources of information, culture enrichment and entertainment through the target language;

  5. To encourage and equip pupils to consider participating in social and cultural activities which may involve some use of the target language;

Leaving Cert examination:


Leaving Certificate German follows a common syllabus framework for the teaching and examining of modern languages in the Leaving Certificate.
The syllabus aims to develop learners’ communicative skills in the German, to develop their strategies for effective language learning and raise their awareness of cultural, social and political diversity.

Assessment is by means of a written examination, and an aural and oral examination at two levels, Ordinary level and Higher level.
